A. Recitals

1. Fresh Lawn contracts with Contractor to perform lawn, gardening, planting, mowing and related landscape services (the “Services”) at Customers’ homes or businesses.

2. Contractor engages in the business of performing the Services and can provide those Services as more specifically defined in a “Work Order” (as defined in Section E) provided to Contractor by Fresh Lawn pursuant to its customers’ requests. Contractor represents and warrants that it has the expertise, experience, and tools to perform the lawn services assigned to it in the Work Order.

3. This Agreement shall become effective as of the Effective Date, and shall apply on a job-by-job basis pursuant to Work Orders accepted by Contractor. This Agreement may be terminated by either party hereto for any reason whatsoever by providing written notice to the other party at least three (3) calendar days prior to the effective date of such termination. Fresh Lawn shall not owe the Contractor compensation for any services provided by the Contractor following the effective date of such termination.

B. Status as Independent Contractor

1. The Contractor hereby acknowledges that he/she is an independent contractor in relation to Fresh Lawn, and said Contractor is in no way to be considered a servant, agent, partner, or an employee of Fresh Lawn, and acknowledges that it is not engaged in a joint venture, partnership, or in any other manner affiliated with Fresh Lawn other than as an independent contractor. It is hereby acknowledged by the Contractor that the Contractor shall have the sole responsibility to control the details of the work performed in accordance with the Work Order and to exert its/his/her individual supervision over any of the employees, associates, and subcontractors of Contractor to perform the work and services required by the Work Order and as otherwise directed by Fresh Lawn.

C. Independent Contractor’s Obligations

1. JOB PERFORMANCE. It is the responsibility of the Contractor to provide the Services in a satisfactory manner and to provide good quality work for the customers of Fresh Lawn. Contractor acknowledges that Contractor has the sole responsibility to fix any issues or problems Contractor causes that arise from Contractor’s failure to perform in accordance with the Work Order upon twenty-four (24) hours’ notice identifying such deficiency.

a. Contractor is free to accept or reject work.

b. Fresh Lawn will not provide Contractor with any equipment in carrying out the duties and responsibilities in supplying the lawn services as required in the Work Order. It is the sole responsibility of Contractor to provide transportation to the job sites, hire its own personnel or subcontractors, and provide, at minimum, the following items and equipment in good working order to provide the Services to the standards expected by Fresh Lawn’s customers:

1. Gas Lawn Mower with a sharp blade and a bag;

2. Gas Weed eater and/or edger;

3. Gas Blower;

4. Gas Hedge Trimmers;

5. Safety Goggles; and

6. A computer, tablet, or smart phone to enable the Contractor to send and receive email, take photos, and utilize Fresh Lawn’s electronic contractor application.

c. Contractor is free to decide how to perform work that complies with a customer’s expectations as specified in a “Work Order.” Fresh Lawn shall have no right or authority to direct or control the Contractor or his/her employees or sub-contractors with respect to the performance of the Contractor's duties under this Agreement, or with respect to any other matter, except as otherwise provided by this Agreement. It is understood and agreed that Fresh Lawn is interested only in the results pursuant to customer’s requested service and performance date as requested in the work order to be achieved by the Contractor under this Agreement; the manner and method of performing all duties and services of the Contractor under this Agreement and achieving the desired results shall be under the exclusive control of the Contractor according to the Contractor’s particular skills and expertise.

d. The Contractor shall provide the services and any associated tangible items as set forth in this Agreement. The Contractor shall further be responsible for maintaining and furnishing, at his/her own expense, a place of work, and any tools, supplies, apparel, facilities, equipment, and appropriate communications devices and services required for the Contractor to render his/her services to Fresh Lawn hereunder.

e. The Contractor may, in his/her sole discretion and without first consulting with Fresh Lawn, hire persons to assist him/her in performing the services required hereunder. Any and all such persons hired by the Contractor to assist in performing such services shall be considered employees or sub-contractors of the Contractor, and, conversely, no person, sub-contractor, or agent hired by the Contractor shall be considered an employee of Fresh Lawn, unless specifically indicated otherwise in an agreement signed by all parties. The parties acknowledge and agree that Fresh Lawn will not have the authority to hire, fire, supervise, control, or manage any of the Contractor’s employees. Upon request, the Contractor shall immediately provide Fresh Lawn with proof of disability, workers’ compensation, unemployment compensation, and/or general liability insurance, as applicable, covering such employees.

f. The Contractor represents and warrants that (s)he is not, and does not at any time under this Agreement, expect that (s)he will become, economically dependent on Fresh Lawn, and further represents and warrants that (s)he is in business for him/herself and not for Fresh Lawn. Nevertheless, as a material term of this Agreement, in the event that under this Agreement, the Contractor does become, or anticipates that (s)he will become, economically dependent upon Fresh Lawn, (s)he shall so notify Fresh Lawn in writing within three (3) business days of such development. The Contractor acknowledges and agrees that his/her failure to provide such written notice shall be deemed a material breach of this Agreement.

2. SAFETY. To help provide for the safety of those performing work under this agreement, the Contractor agrees that anyone performing the Services shall wear appropriate clothing at all times. The items to be worn are to be determined in the sole discretion of the Independent Contractor in relation to the Services to be performed, but should project a professional appearance. The items to be worn are at the sole discretion of the Contractor with regard to prevailing standards of safety and professionalism in the industry.

3. LEGAL COMPLIANCE. The Contractor agrees to comply in all material respects with all applicable local, state and federal laws, rules and regulations now or hereafter in force. The methods of compliance are to be determined in the sole discretion of the Independent Contractor.

4. CONFLICTS WITH CUSTOMERS. The Contractor shall refer conflicts with Customers concerning any issue (e.g., pets, fences, etc.) to the customer directly by using the Fresh Lawn app, or with assistance from a dispatcher, so it can be addressed further.

5. PHOTOGRAPHS. Contractor shall take photographs of the Customer’s lawn once the work is completed. Should the Customer request that Contractor not take photographs, Contractor shall stop and delete the photographs, if so requested. In the event that photographs are permitted, Contractor agrees:

a. Not to take pictures facing the Customer’s house wherein the interior of the house is visible in the photograph.

b. Never take pictures of the interior of the Customer’s house.

6. INSURANCE. Contractor shall carry and maintain automobile insurance and shall provide evidence that such insurance is in full force and effect by furnishing Fresh Lawn with a Certificate of Insurance at the inception of this agreement and when reasonably requested by Fresh Lawn and no less than on a quarterly basis. Because Fresh Lawn is not your employer, it shall not furnish you with insurance. The Contractor is solely responsible, to the extent required by applicable law, rule, or regulation or as requested by Fresh Lawn, for securing his/her own disability, unemployment compensation, general liability, and/or other insurance coverage, and for obtaining workers’ compensation insurance and training, for him/herself and others, as necessary.

D. Contractor Payment

1. In exchange for performing the services described in the Work Order, Fresh Lawn shall pay Contractor a flat rate subject to sale taxes withheld and any other deductions as described in Section D(4).

2. If Contractor finds an error in the amount it received in Compensation, it must notify Fresh Lawn of the alleged error in writing within THIRTY (30) of its occurrence. The failure of Contractor to notify Fresh Lawn within this time shall serve as a WAIVER of any claims of Contractor.

3. PAYMENT. Fresh Lawn will have checks made payable to the Contractor after 5:00 p.m., C.S.T., each Friday, subject to Texas and Federal holidays

a. To receive Contractor Payment, Contractor must complete all jobs for that day and the preceding week with pictures showing the completion of the jobs.

b. Should the Contractor be unable to perform Friday’s work or show proof of completion before issuance the payments, Fresh Lawn will pay the Contractor the following Monday after 3:00 p.m. upon verification with the required or adequate proof of completion.

4. TAXES. Fresh Lawn will collect all payments from the customers.

a. It will collect all applicable sales taxes and remit said taxes to the Texas Comptroller of Public Accounts.

b. Fresh Lawn will issue a resale Sales Tax Certificate to the Contractor. Fresh Lawn will deduct the applicable 8.25% from payments made to the Contractor.

5. Fresh Lawn will send an IRS form 1099 to the Contractor to the address supplied above. Fresh Lawn is not responsible for paying Federal Taxes on behalf of Contractor and the Contractor’s employees.

6. The Contractor shall provide Fresh Lawn with a signed and completed IRS Form W-9 upon execution of this Agreement. Payment by Fresh Lawn will be made to the individual named on the IRS Form W-9. The Contractor hereby agrees to notify Fresh Lawn immediately upon any change of taxpayer information found on the IRS Form W-9.

7. Fresh Lawn shall not withhold any taxes due by the Contractor from any payment it makes to the Contractor under this Agreement, nor shall Fresh Lawn make any contributions to any federal, state, or local agency with respect to such payments on behalf of the Contractor. Fresh Lawn shall report the payments made to the Contractor hereunder on an IRS Form 1099. The Contractor agrees and represents that, except as otherwise required in writing by the Internal Revenue Service, the Contractor shall be responsible for paying, when due, all taxes, including estimated taxes, incurred, imposed, or assessed, as a result of the Contractor’s receipt of fees from Fresh Lawn pursuant to this Agreement, including but not limited to, income taxes, self-employment taxes, federal and/or state taxes.

The Contractor hereby acknowledges that neither (s)he nor his/her employees shall: (i) be eligible to participate in any of Fresh Lawn’s employee benefit plans, fringe benefit programs, group insurance arrangements, or other similar plans, programs, or arrangements maintained by Fresh Lawn for the benefit of its employees, (ii) ever claim that (s)he is or was eligible for, or entitled to, any benefits under any such plan, program, or arrangement, or (iii) receive any statutory benefit that Fresh Lawn makes available to its employees, including but not limited to workers’ compensation, Social Security, or unemployment compensation coverage.

b. The Customer’s address;

c. The flat rate paid to Contractor for the work order if accepted by Contractor;

b. A $100 penalty will be assessed to Contractor should it fail to provide 7 days written notice prior to cancelling this Agreement. The penalty shall be assessed from any monies owed to Contractor and shall be due to Fresh Lawn as liquidated damages.

Notwithstanding the above, either Fresh Lawn or the Contractor shall have the right to cancel this Agreement immediately if the other party commits a criminal or illegal act, embezzlement, fraud, willful conduct that is materially injurious to the other Party, and/or a material breach of this Agreement.
